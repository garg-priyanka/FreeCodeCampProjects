Narendra Modi

Picture of Narendra Modi depicting India rising with progress and development

    Biography

  • 1950 - Born to Hiraba and Damodardas Modi in Vadnagar in the state of Gujarat (Mehsana district)
  • 1965 - Modi helped his father sell tea at the Vadnagar railway station and later ran a tea stall with his brother near a bus terminus. When India-Pakistan War Breaks Out he serves tea at Mehsana Railway Station to Jawans
  • 1967 - Engaged while still a child to Jashodaben, a girl from a family who lived close by, Modi rejected the arranged marriage at the same time he graduated from high school.[31] The resulting familial tensions contributed to his decision to leave home. He travels across India including the Himalayas, Rishikesh, Ramakrishna Mission
  • 1972 - became a full-time pracharak (campaigner) for the RSS
  • 1975 - Emergency is imposed. Narendra Modi gets fully involved in anti-Emergency struggle.Modi was appointed general secretary of the "Gujarat Lok Sangharsh Samiti"
  • 1987 -Joins BJP as Organisation Secretary of Gujarat Unit.
  • 1995 - Moves to Delhi as National Secretary.
  • 2001 - Sworn in as the Chief Minister of Gujarat.
  • 2012 - Leads BJP to yet another victory in the Gujarat Assembly Polls. Becomes CM for 4th time.
  • 2013 - BJP declares Narendra Modi as the PM candidate of the Party for 2014 Lok Sabha Elections.
  • 2014 -Sworn in as Prime Minister of India
